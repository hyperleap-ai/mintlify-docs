title: "Create and Configure Persona"
description: "Learn how to create and set up your AI personas in Hyperleap AI"

Follow these steps to create and configure a Persona in Hyperleap AI:

## Prerequisites

Before creating Personas, ensure you have:

- Access to [Studio](https://studio.hyperleapai.com)
- Sufficient credits in your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Started">
    There are two ways to create a Persona:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Start from Scratch">
        Start creating your Persona from Persona Playground

        <Frame>
          <img
            src="/images/personas/persona-pg.png"
            alt="Creating a new persona from persona playground"
            width="800"
          />
        </Frame>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Use Template">
        Choose pre-configured Persona from Persona library

        <Frame>
          <img
            src="/images/personas/persona-library.png"
            alt="Selecting a Persona from Persona library"
            width="800"
          />
        </Frame>
        <Frame>
          <img
            src="/images/personas/import-persona-library.png"
            alt="import Persona from Persona library"
            width="800"
          />
        </Frame>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

  </Step>

 <Step title="Define Behaviour">
    Define or update your Persona's behavior. You can include dynamic variables (e.g. `{{variable_name}}`) that will be replaced with actual values when the Persona is used.

    - Use `{{variable_name}}` syntax for replacements

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/personas/enter-input.png"
        alt="Defining persona behaviour"
        width="800"
      />
    </Frame>

</Step>

  <Step title="Configure Persona">
    Configure the core aspects of your persona:
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Set AI Model" icon="microchip">
        Choose the AI model that best fits your use case:
        
        <Frame>
          <img 
            src="/images/personas/set-model.png" 
            alt="Setting AI model" 
            width="800"
          />
        </Frame>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Add Sources" icon="database">
        Connect your data Sources to improve the accuracy and relevance of AI responses. This allows the AI to reference up-to-date information and context, ensuring more informed interactions.
        - You can upload documents, website URLs, or text content
        <Note>
        To learn more about Sources, check out the [Sources](/guides/sources) guide.
        </Note>

        <Frame>
          <img
            src="/images/personas/sources.png"
            alt="Adding sources"
            width="800"
          />
        </Frame>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Adjust Configuration Parameters" icon="sliders">
        Fine-tune your Persona parameters:
        <Frame>
          <img
            src="/images/personas/adjust-configuration.png"
            alt="Adjusting configurations"
            width="800"
          />
        </Frame>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Access Template Library" icon="file-import">
        Access our template library at any point during configuration:
        - View available templates by category
        - Preview template behaviors
        - Apply template settings to your current persona

        <Frame>
          <img
            src="/images/personas/import-persona.png"
            alt="Importing Personas"
            width="800"
          />
        </Frame>
        <Note>
        This is an alternative way to access templates if you didn't start with one. Your existing configurations will be overridden.
        </Note>
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>

  </Step>

<Step title="Test Interactions">
  Validate your Persona's behavior in the playground: 
  - Test different scenarios
  - Verify knowledge accuracy 
  - Check response consistency
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/personas/test-behaviour.png"
      alt="Testing Persona"
      width="800"
    />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Compare Models">
  Compare the performance of different AI models: 
  - View response quality 
  - Adjust configurations 
  - Analyze response time
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/personas/compare-test.png"
      alt="Configure and compare models"
      width="800"
    />
  </Frame>
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/personas/compare-response.png"
      alt="Compare response"
      width="800"
    />
  </Frame>
</Step>

  <Step title="Save and Publish">
    Finalize your Persona configuration:
    - Save as draft to continue editing and share with your team later
    - Publish to make it available to your team
    
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/personas/save-draft.png" 
        alt="Save and publish Persona" 
        width="800"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Users on the Free plan can create up to 10 Personas. Upgrade your plan to
  create more Personas and access additional features.
</Note>
