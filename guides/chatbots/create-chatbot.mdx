Learn how to create and configure AI chatbots for your website

## Prerequisites

Before creating chatbots, ensure you have:
- Access to [Studio](https://studio.hyperleapai.com)
- An active Chatbot subscription
- Website access for deployment

<Note>
    You have to [whitelist](https://studio.hyperleapai.com/manage-organization) the domain of your website in the organization settings to use the chatbot.
</Note>
