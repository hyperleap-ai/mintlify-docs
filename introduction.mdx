## Features

Here are some of the features that Hyperleap AI offers:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Personas API" icon="message">
    Integrate Conversational APIs with built-in state and memory management, allowing you to focus on delivering impactful use cases. [Learn
    more](/guides/personas/personas)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Prompts API" icon="terminal">
    Launch Generative AI in your products in days. Hyperleap AI manages the entire lifecycle—starting from prompt engineering to observability and more in one central place. [Learn more](/guides/prompts/prompts)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI Chatbots" icon="comments">
    Turn your website into a lead-catching machine with smart AI chatbots that talk to visitors around the clock. Our chatbots fit your brand's style, answer questions instantly, and collect visitor information while you sleep—and you can set it all up in minutes without any tech skills needed. [Learn
    more](/introduction)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Chat Studio" icon="comment-dots">
  Chat with any top models like OpenAI, Google Gemini, and more from one central place with ease.
  [Learn more](/introduction)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tools" icon="wrench">
    Use AI tools that automate your business - from marketing to sales to operations and more. Take charge of work with over 500+ tools included or customize your own.
 [Learn more](/introduction)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Assistants" icon="robot">
    Create AI Co-pilots tailored for your business workflows. Start right away with over 150+ assistants, or custom create your own in minutes — based on your team's unique needs. [Learn more](/introduction)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sources" icon="brain">
    Add business documents, websites, and text to power AI that speaks your language. Sources work across all our features. Our advanced retrieval engine (RAG) automatically processes and indexes your content, so your AI delivers contextually relevant responses. [Learn more](/guides/sources)
